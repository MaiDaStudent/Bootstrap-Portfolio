A showcase of my web design projects, focusing on UX/UI, content, and clean code.

Featured Projects

Miss OTB Website Design

I designed a vibrant and dynamic website for Miss OTB, a charismatic drag queen who needed a fun, colorful, and functional online presence. The site reflects her bold personality, featuring eye-catching visuals and intuitive navigation. The primary goal? Get her booked for gigs effortlessly while showcasing her unique style.

ØK Club Website Design

I designed a modern and intuitive website for ØK Club, a long-standing community for former employees of ØK. The goal was to create a clean, professional, and easy-to-navigate platform that reflects the club’s heritage while being highly accessible for its members, many of whom are elderly. The result is a user-friendly interface that ensures all members can stay connected, access updates, and engage with the club effortlessly.

EcoVaner App Design

For EcoVaner, I designed an app that transforms sustainable habits into a fun and engaging experience. The app works as a gamified checklist, encouraging users to adopt eco-friendly habits in an interactive way. The challenge was to create a visually appealing yet lightweight design that aligns with the app’s sustainability goals. To achieve this, I used dark colors to reduce screen energy consumption and iconography instead of heavy images, ensuring an eco-conscious and accessible user experience.
